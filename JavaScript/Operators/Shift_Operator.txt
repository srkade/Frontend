Shift Operators:-
○ Two variations - left and right shift

○ left shift: shift bits in left direction for specified value

○ right shift: shift bits in right direction for specified value

○ Example:
var a=8,b=2;
console.log("a<<b : "+ (a<<b));
console.log("a>>b : "+ (a>>b));
Output
a<<b : 32
a>>b : 2
